:golf: **Change Log BattleGOLF v0.91 (March 21, 2026) :golf: **

- Introduced new camera system.
- Created cinematic intro for each hole while displaying scoresheet.
- New preview camera to improve visibility on predicted shot landing position.
- New ball tracking camera after hitting the ball.
- Camera transitions when interacting with the ball, changing clubs and power, and hitting the ball.
- New player position handling after the ball stops rolling.
- Improved visual transitions between swing phases.
- New VFX to improve visibility of the flag at far distances from the cup.
- Changed panel location indicator for each hole from group-based to player-based. Worldicon removed after the player leaves the tee-off.
- Ball trail VFX simplified and refactored.
- Fixed a glitch where the Power Hit ball trail rotation was not relative to the ball velocity and direction.
- Fixed a glitch where the Next Turn HighlightedWorldLogMessage was displayed for all players instead of the group players.
- Changed the hole panel placement on Hole 4.

**Known glitches**

- Ball VFX does not perfectly align with the ball SpatialObject. It looks like position updates are computed differently at runtime.
- Screen fade in/out sometimes flicker. This appears to be a result of the way player camera changes are handled, when combined with my custom fade-in transition UI.

:golf:  **Change Log BattleGOLF v0.93 (April 3, 2026) ** :golf: 

- Removed the stairs at the spawn of Hole 1 and replaced with a ramp, leading players to the tee (to the left) instead of the flag (forward).
- Reworked the hole intro camera to make the path much smoother.
- Ball hit confirmation button changed from OnButtonUp to OnButtonDown, to help with timing and feedback.
- Increased the base Power Hit inventory amount from 1 to 3, so players start with more Power Hit uses.
- Increased the flag cup radius from a very small realistic value to 0.25 meters, which makes hole detection more forgiving.
- Greatly improved the camera angle during ball setup from the tee off for dog legs, such as Hole 4 and Hole 6.
- Added "Out of Bounds" surface type. This ensures smoother ball and camera handling for dog legs.
- Redefined boundaries for Hole 4 and Hole 6.
- Improved out-of-bounds aiming feedback.
- Adjusted tee box club restrictions on some specific holes.
- Improved Chinese language persistence when player state is reset or rebuilt.
- Improved some UI cleanup and rebuilding.
- Added a group unique ID safety check during player-related flow, so logic can stop if the player’s group changed while the function was still running.
- Adjusted the green view world icon placement height.
